Our Product Range

We offer 25+ varieties of handmade ladoos, each formulated to meet specific health needs and preferences:

Traditional Ladoos

Classic favorites like Peanut, Sesame, Coconut, and Date ladoos crafted using time-tested traditional recipes.

Herbal Ladoos

Infused with Ayurvedic herbs like Ashwagandha, Tulsi, Moringa, Beetroot, and Shatavari for targeted wellness.

Millet Ladoos

Gluten-free ancient grain ladoos made with Ragi and Millet for sustainable nutrition and digestive health.

Baby-Friendly Ladoos

Specially formulated from 8 months onwards with gentle ingredients, easy digestion, and no allergens.
