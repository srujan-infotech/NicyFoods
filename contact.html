Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

You can place an order by calling us at +91 8263001410 or emailing nicyfoods5@gmail.com. Use the contact form to get in touch with your specific requirements.

What is the minimum order quantity?

We accept orders of any quantity. For bulk orders or special requests, please contact us directly for best pricing and availability.

Do you deliver outside Pune?

Currently, we primarily deliver in and around Pune. For inquiries about delivery to other areas, please contact us to discuss shipping options.

Are your products suitable for diabetics?

While we use natural jaggery instead of refined sugar, we recommend consulting with your healthcare provider. Our millet varieties are also a good low-glycemic option.

What about quality and freshness?

All our products are handmade and delivered fresh. We don't use preservatives, so products should be consumed within 7-10 days for optimal taste and nutritional value.

Can you customize orders?

Yes! We can customize orders based on your preferences. Contact us to discuss custom varieties, packaging, or bulk requirements.

Interested in Bulk Orders or Partnership?

We offer special pricing for bulk orders, corporate gifts, and business partnerships. Contact us directly to discuss your requirements and get a custom quote.
